BMC Remedy, now known as [[BMC Helix ISTM]], is a comprehensive IT service management (ITSM) solution designed to help organizations manage their IT services and support processes. It provides a suite of applications for incident management, problem management, change management, asset management, and more.

Key features of BMC Remedy include:

1. Incident Management: Helps track and resolve incidents efficiently to minimize disruption to services.
2. Change Management: Facilitates the planning and implementation of changes to IT services while minimizing risk.
3. Problem Management: Identifies and manages the root causes of incidents to prevent future occurrences.
4. Asset Management: Manages IT assets throughout their lifecycle to optimize usage and reduce costs.
5. Self-Service Portal: Allows users to submit requests and track their status, improving user experience.

BMC Remedy is often used by large organizations to standardize IT processes, improve service delivery, and enhance overall productivity. The platform can be customized and integrated with other tools and systems, making it flexible for various IT environments.
