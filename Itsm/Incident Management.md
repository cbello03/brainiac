**When creating a new incident in [[BMC Helix ISTM]] you'll typically need to fill out the following fields:**

### **Basic Incident Information:**

- **Incident Number:** Automatically generated by the system.
- **Incident Type:** Categorizes the incident (e.g., hardware, software, network).
- **Priority:** Indicates the urgency of the incident (e.g., critical, high, medium, low).
- **Impact:** Describes the potential business impact of the incident.
- **Affected Component:** Specifies the specific IT component involved (e.g., server, application).
- **Reported By:** The person who reported the incident.
- **Assigned To:** The technician responsible for resolving the incident.
- **Status:** The current state of the incident (e.g., open, in progress, resolved, closed).

### **Additional Details:**

- **Description:** A detailed explanation of the problem.
- **Symptoms:** The observed effects of the incident.
- **Cause:** The suspected root cause of the problem (if known).
- **Workaround:** Temporary solutions or steps taken to mitigate the impact.
- **Due Date:** The target date for resolving the incident.
- **Related Incidents:** Links to other related incidents.
- **Configuration Items:** Associates the incident with specific IT assets.
- **Knowledge Base Articles:** References to relevant knowledge base articles.
- **Attachments:** Files or documents related to the incident.

**Note:** The specific fields and their required or optional nature may vary depending on your organization's ITSM configuration and customization.

By providing accurate and complete information in these fields, you can help ensure that the incident is resolved efficiently and effectively.
